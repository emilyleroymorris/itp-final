Emily Morris - Final Project Proposal

Topic

This website will be designed to showcase my work, experience, and potential as an employee. As a graduating senior, I'll need a distinct online presence to begin developing a personal brand to make it in the competitive world of publishing. Although I don't have much personal experience now, I hope to develop the bare bones of a portfolio and online resume to attract potential employers.

Audience

The audience will be any potential employers or people looking to hire a freelance copywriter or copyeditor.

Design & Style

The design will be relatively simple to allow for my work and words to shine through, but the color palette will be bright and warm. The colors will also have a more vintage tint because writing and literate, at its best, is timeless, so I want to focus on classic colors.

Inspiration

I will be choosing the websites of a few established authors, as well as a copyeditor and a more recent author, as inspiration for my webpage.

  • Barbara Kingsolver
  • Artem Mozgovoy
